Creating glossary

  1. Enter a topic name in the Topic input field and click the Save button.
  2. Select the added topic in the menu selector and click the New 4 button. 4 new words will be added to the words database as "L" and "E" showing on the screen where to add Latvian and English words.
  3. Replace L and E with new words in aprropriate languages.
  4. Click the Record button to record  the pronunciation of the words. Click the Play button to check the pronunciation quality. You can record the words any number of times.
  5. Click the Save buttons to save the added data.
  6. Click 'New 4' to create additional 4 words.

Editing glossary

  1. Select a topic in the menu selector and click OK.
  2. Find a necessary word(s) by using the Next and Back buttons.
  3. Change the words and click the Save button to save the changes. The Next and Back butoons also save the made changes.
